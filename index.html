Outdoor Activity

Field Session

The afternoon runs at Plan de l'Aiguille (2,308 m) — mid-station of the Aiguille du Midi cable car. Dress for alpine conditions: cold, wind, and possible snow even in late April.

  • Warm mid-layer
  • Waterproof shell jacket
  • Waterproof boots
  • Warm hat & gloves
  • Sunscreen & sunglasses
  • Personal avalanche transceiver
  • Small daypack
  • Cable car ticket

Getting There

Travel Guidelines

  • Visa: EU and Schengen delegates do not need a visa for France. Check requirements at france-visas.gouv.fr.
  • Nearest airport: Geneva International Airport (GVA), about 1h15 from Chamonix by car/shuttle.
  • Alternative airports: Lyon-Saint Exupery (~2h) and Chambery (~1h30).
  • From Geneva to Chamonix: direct shuttles (SwissTours, AlpyTransfers, Flixbus, BlablacarBus) or train via Saint-Gervais-les-Bains-Le Fayet (~2h total).
  • Electricity in France: 220V, type C plugs.
